Thus we consider both thick discs and CMCs formed in an early disc with a large scale height compared to that of the thin disc. The dynamically ‚Äúcold component‚Äù is made of the thin disc and the gas disc (for which the atomic gas mass is a lower mass boundary).

Bulge formation is a secondary step involving angular momentum transfer in a disc that is already thick from high turbulence.
